# Export and Review

Before submitting, verify:

- The diagram title, team name, version, and date are visible.
- AWS Cloud, Region, VPC, Availability Zone, and Public Subnet boundaries are clear.
- Dashboard, Logging, Detection/Response, and Governance groups are color-coded.
- Approval, forensic collection, snapshot, isolation, and notification flows are labeled.
